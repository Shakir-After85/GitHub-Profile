I then switched my focus to my DJ and event business and went to school for Recording Arts and Technology. From there is when I decided to try my hand in learning how to program. This journey has been about 3-4 years for me and I started off all over the place, trying to learn multiple languages at once, watching too many videos and not actually writing code. Now I am currently more focused and structured with my learning process and looking forward to continue learning more and growing in this field. I am currently familiar with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, BootStrap and Python. My goal is to continue to learn and build my skills as a Software Engineer/Developer and a technologist in general.
